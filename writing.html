Poetry

Here's a beautiful piece of poetry that you can enjoy:

A thousand things can feel like love.
You can make my heart flutter and bring it calm.
You're my beautiful beginning and painful end,
My everything, my love, not my forever but my infinity.
My happiness you give me, my loudest laughs.
You're my cute dynamite, my powerhouse.
My beautiful dream on cloud seven.
You're my safe reality, my peace and calm.
You're my only 'please don't sleep' good night.
To have the best day, but I want you with me, good morning.
I tell you See you later, but really be wanting to see you again and hug you again, kiss you again.
You’ve changed meanings and brought intentions to phrases.
You’ve altered my language and meanings to rather pure intentions I hide within myself.
I love you forever if it's the longest time sequence.
I love you for everything.
I love you for being mine.
